Melody4U target image style:
- High-impact commercial social media poster, not a generic AI illustration.
- Premium cinematic advertising look with a finished campaign-poster feel.
- Vertical 9:16 composition for Facebook, TikTok, Instagram Reels and Stories.
- Strong central subject, clear foreground / midground / background layers and obvious visual hierarchy.
- Bold large headline typography that is readable on a mobile screen.
- Neon purple, magenta, blue and dark stadium/night tones as the Melody4U brand energy.
- Emotional fan, gift or remembrance moment connected to the event.
- Smartphone clearly visible as the Melody4U product/action anchor, showing a music gift or personal wish concept.
- Glowing sound waves, music notes, waveform UI, light streaks, particles, glow, lens flare and depth.
- Realistic or cinematic semi-realistic rendering with dramatic lighting and high contrast.
- Energetic but clean composition with a clear call-to-action/product feeling, without overcrowding.
- Facebook / TikTok / Instagram Reels ready and suitable for manual social posting.

Avoid:
- Flat vector illustration.
- Childish cartoon style.
- Simple flat orange poster.
- Empty composition.
- Weak typography.
- Tiny unreadable text.
- Generic stock poster.
- Bland single-object composition.
- Official logos.
- Official badges.
- Real athlete faces.
- Celebrity likenesses.
- Exact trademarked visual identity.
- Fake official tournament branding.
- Too much text.
- Graphic or disturbing imagery.
