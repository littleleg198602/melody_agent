Create a weekly overview for Melody4U social media images.

Use web search to find current events for the target week. Focus on sports, motorsport, football, hockey, tennis, cycling, important international days, and Czech-relevant events where possible.

Return valid JSON only. Do not include markdown fences or commentary. Return 5 to 7 strong events, not 10 weak events.

The model may generate only candidate events. The script is responsible for week, generated_at, status normalization, and final filtering.

Event priority rules:
- Priority 5: Czech national team matches; major global sports events with Czech relevance; Formula 1 Grand Prix weekends; MotoGP weekends; Ice Hockey World Championship / major hockey events; FIFA World Cup / UEFA Euro / Champions League level events; Wimbledon / Grand Slam tennis; Olympics / World Championships level events.
- Priority 4: major international sports events without Czech relevance; widely known international days that fit gifts, emotions, family, music, love, friendship, children, mothers, fathers, teachers, holidays; major motorsport events.
- Priority 3: locally relevant Czech events; general seasonal events; cultural days suitable for Melody4U social content.
- Priority 1-2: niche national championships from other countries; weakly relevant holidays; events without clear social media or Melody4U emotional value.

Selection rules:
- Prefer Czech-relevant or mainstream recognizable events.
- Do not fill the list with weak niche events just to reach a count.
- Avoid niche national championships from other countries unless there is clear Czech relevance or strong Melody4U emotional value.
- Avoid weak holidays that do not fit gifts, emotion, music, family, friendship, or celebrations.

Every event must contain:
- date
- category
- title
- note
- priority from 1 to 5
- generate_image boolean
- status set to "waiting"
- czech_relevance: "high", "medium", or "low"
- melody4u_score from 1 to 5
- marketing_angle, for example "family watching sport together", "personal greeting before a big match", "gift for a fan", "weekend emotion", or "celebration day"
- why_selected explaining why the event is useful for Melody4U

Content safety rules:
- Avoid official logos, team badges, copyrighted mascots, real athlete likenesses, celebrity likenesses, and trademarked visual identity.
- Prefer symbolic cinematic event descriptions.
- Use symbolic scenes instead of exact protected branding.
